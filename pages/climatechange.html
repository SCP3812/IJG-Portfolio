Climate Change Presentation

Presentation regarding my research on Climate Change and its causes and effects. Essentially, I was tasked with creating a research presentation exploring the causes and effects of climate change. I read academic sources and surmized the information into an easily understood presentation folder. I used graphic design principles to help present the information in an easily understood manner. I then presented the poster to a wide audience of all sorts.
